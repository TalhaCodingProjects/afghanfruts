Cumin

Cumin is a popular spice known for its warm, earthy flavor and distinctive aroma. Rich in antioxidants and essential oils, it offers various health benefits, including aiding digestion, enhancing immunity, and providing anti-inflammatory properties. Commonly used in cuisines worldwide, cumin is a key ingredient in dishes like curries, stews, and spice blends, adding depth and richness to meals.
